DIVIDED SLAB METHOD

Split your electricity
bill fairly.

One shared meter. Multiple users. Each person climbs their own slab ladder — paying the cheapest rate first, regardless of how much others consume.

⚖️

Divide slabs equally

Each master slab's units are divided by the number of users, giving everyone an equal "share" per rate tier.

🪜

Climb your own ladder

Every user starts at the cheapest rate and progresses upward only based on their own consumption.

🔒

Fixed charges split equally

Meter rent, ED charges, and customer fees are shared equally — independent of how much each user consumed.
